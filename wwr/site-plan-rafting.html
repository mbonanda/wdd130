Overview

Full Moon Adventure Co., founded one year ago, has quickly become a leading name in the world of white-water rafting. Nestled in the heart of scenic landscapes, our company provides exhilarating and memorable adventures for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Purpose

At Full Moon Adventure Co., we are passionate about delivering unparalleled white-water experiences that combine the thrill of the rapids with the beauty of the great outdoors. Our mission is to create lasting memories, foster a love for adventure, and promote environmental stewardship."

Audience

• Tailored packages for individuals, families, and corporate groups. • Options for half-day, full-day, and multi-day adventures. • Expertly guided tours catering to various skill levels. • Diverse range of rivers, each offering a unique challenge and stunning scenery. • Highly trained and certified guides prioritizing safety without compromising on excitement. • State-of-the-art equipment and rigorous safety protocols. • Commitment to sustainable and eco-friendly practices. • Educational programs promoting environmental awareness among participants.
