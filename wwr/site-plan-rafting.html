Overview

Purpose

Through river rafting, you are mostly using your upper body to paddle through the water, which tones your arms, back, shoulders and even strengthens your ability to think.

Audience

We all need a little adventure in our lives. Step out of your comfort zone, feel your adrenaline flow while the river rushes around you. Whitewater rafting is a thrilling, adventure that really makes you feel alive, puts a smile on your face and truly makes you happy
